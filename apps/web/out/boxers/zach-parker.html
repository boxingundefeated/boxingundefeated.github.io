Biography

When it comes to rising stars in the super middleweight division, one name that cannot be left out is Zach Parker. Born on June 2, 1994, this British boxer has caught the attention of fans and experts alike with his remarkable talent and potential.

Standing at an impressive 183 centimeters tall with a reach of 188 centimeters, Parker possesses a physical advantage that allows him to dominate his opponents in the ring. His orthodox stance further enhances his ability to deliver powerful and accurate punches.

Competing in the super middleweight weight class, Parker has proven himself to be a force to be reckoned with. His skill, dedication, and determination have propelled him through a series of victories, earning him a reputation as a credible and exciting contender in the division.

Despite his youth, Parker has already accumulated an impressive professional record, showcasing his exceptional boxing abilities. With each fight, he continues to impress, leaving spectators in awe of his lightning-fast combinations and well-timed counterpunches.
