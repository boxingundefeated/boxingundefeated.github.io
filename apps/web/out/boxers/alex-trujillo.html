Biography

Alex Trujillo, commonly known as "Alexander the Great," is a formidable boxer who has made a significant impact in the sport. Born on August 28, 1974, Trujillo has established himself as a force to be reckoned with in the welterweight division, showcasing his skills and determination inside the ring.

Standing at a height of 175cm and possessing a reach of 173cm, Trujillo utilizes his physical attributes to his advantage. Being an orthodox boxer, he adopts a traditional stance that allows him to effectively execute his power punches and defensive maneuvers.

With a weight of 72.57kg, Trujillo competes in the welterweight weight class, which is known for its highly competitive nature. This weight class has produced some of the most legendary fighters in boxing history, and Trujillo aims to leave his mark among them.

Trujillo's journey in boxing spans over two decades, where he has faced numerous challenges and triumphs.
