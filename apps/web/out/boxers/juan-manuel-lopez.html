Biography

Juan Manuel Lopez, also known by his nickname "JuanMa", is a highly accomplished professional boxer hailing from Puerto Rico. Born on June 30, 1983, JuanMa has been involved in the sport for over two decades, making a name for himself with his exceptional skills and relentless determination.

Throughout his career, JuanMa has competed in multiple weight classes, showcasing his versatility and ability to adapt to different challenges. He has competed in the Super Bantamweight, Featherweight, Super Featherweight, and Lightweight divisions, demonstrating his willingness to test his skills against formidable opponents across the spectrum of weight classes.

One of JuanMa's most notable achievements was becoming a world champion in the Super Bantamweight division. On October 1, 2008, he defeated Daniel Ponce de Leon by technical knockout in the first round, capturing the WBO super bantamweight title.
