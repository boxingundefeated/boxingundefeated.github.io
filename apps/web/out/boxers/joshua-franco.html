Biography

Joshua Franco, also known as "El Profesor," is a rising star in the world of boxing. Born on October 27, 1995, Franco has quickly made a name for himself with his impressive skills and unwavering determination.

Standing at a height of 162cm with a reach of 170cm, Franco may not be the tallest boxer in the ring, but what he lacks in size, he makes up for in heart and technique. His orthodox stance allows him to effectively utilize his strengths and exploit his opponents' weaknesses.

Franco competes in the super flyweight weight class, which is known for its fast-paced and high-intensity bouts. This weight class requires fighters to weigh no more than 115 pounds (52.2 kilograms), ensuring that the competition is fierce and the action is non-stop.

Although still relatively young in his career, Franco's determination and talent have already led him to impressive victories. He has proven time and time again that he is a force to be reckoned with inside the squared circle.

One of the most notable aspects of Franco's fighting style is his ability to adapt to any situation. Whether he's facing a taller opponent with a longer reach or an aggressive brawler, Franco always finds a way to adjust his strategy and come out on top.

But it's not just his in-ring skills that set him apart. Franco's work ethic is second to none. He puts in the hours of training and conditioning necessary to excel in a sport as physically demanding as boxing. His dedication to his craft is evident in every fight he steps into.

While Franco may not yet have reached the pinnacle of his career, he has already achieved significant milestones. His hard work paid off in 2019 when he claimed the NABF super flyweight title with a dominant victory over Oscar Negrete. This win catapulted Franco into the spotlight and solidified his status as a rising star in the super flyweight division.

As Franco continues to climb the ranks, there's no doubt that he will face tough challenges along the way. But with his skill set, determination, and the support of his loyal fans, there's no doubt that he will face those challenges head-on and come out victorious.
