Biography

Marlon Starling, also known as the "Magic Man," is a legendary boxer born on August 29, 1959. Standing at an impressive 185cm and boasting a reach of 73 inches, Starling made a name for himself in the welterweight division with his exceptional skills and unyielding determination.

Throughout his career, Starling displayed remarkable technique, footwork, and ring intelligence that allowed him to outsmart and outmaneuver his adversaries.
