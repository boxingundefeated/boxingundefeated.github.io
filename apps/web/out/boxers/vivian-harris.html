Biography

Meet Vivian Harris, a formidable boxer with a reputation for being vicious inside the ring. Born on June 17, 1978, Harris has been a prominent figure in the boxing world for over two decades. Standing at 180cm tall with an impressive reach of 188cm, he commands attention every time he steps into the squared circle. Known for his orthodox stance, Harris has competed in the Super Lightweight and Welterweight weight classes, showcasing his versatility and skill across multiple divisions.

Hailing from Guyana, Harris began his professional boxing career in 1997 at the age of 19. From the outset, it was clear that he possessed a natural talent and dedication to the sport. With each fight, he honed his skills and gained valuable experience, steadily building a name for himself in the boxing community.

One of the most notable aspects of Harris' career is his nickname, "Vicious." This moniker aptly captures his aggressive fighting style and determination to dominate his opponents. Whether he's unleashing powerful hooks or delivering devastating uppercuts, Harris always leaves it all in the ring, constantly pushing the boundaries of what he can achieve.

Throughout his career, Harris has faced off against some of the biggest names in the boxing world. His ability to persevere in the face of adversity has made him a formidable opponent. His fights are often characterized by intense exchanges, showcasing his skill, resilience, and undying spirit. Harris has proven time and again that he is not one to back down from a challenge.

One of Harris' standout moments came in 2002 when he secured the WBA Super Lightweight title by defeating Diosbelys Hurtado. This victory cemented his place among the top fighters in the division and put the boxing world on notice. Harris showed incredible poise and technique throughout the fight, ultimately emerging victorious and etching his name into the record books.

As an orthodox fighter, Harris boasts a well-rounded skill set that includes powerful punches, quick footwork, and strategic defense. His ability to adapt to different opponents and adjust his tactics accordingly has been a key factor in his success throughout his career.

Throughout the years, Harris has continuously pushed the boundaries of his abilities and strived to leave a lasting impact on the sport of boxing. Despite facing setbacks and challenges along the way, he has remained resilient and has bounced back stronger than ever.

Off the ropes, Harris is known for his humility and genuine love for the sport. Despite his accomplishments, he remains grounded and dedicated to his craft. His passion for boxing shines through in every match, captivating audiences and inspiring aspiring boxers around the world.

In conclusion, Vivian Harris is a force to be reckoned with in the boxing world. With his aggressive fighting style, technical prowess, and unwavering determination, he has left an indelible mark on the sport. As he continues to add to his already impressive legacy, the boxing community eagerly awaits the next chapter in the career of this true warrior.
