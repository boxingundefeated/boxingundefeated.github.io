When it comes to super featherweights, one name that cannot be overlooked is Ian McLeod. Born on June 11, 1969, McLeod has proven himself to be a force to be reckoned with in the boxing ring. Despite not having any widely-known nicknames, Ian McLeod has made a name for himself with his exceptional skills and determination.

With a reach of 183cm, he has the ability to keep his opponents at bay while delivering powerful punches. This combination of height and reach gives him a significant advantage when it comes to strategic positioning and controlling the pace of the fight.

As a super featherweight, McLeod competes in one of the most competitive weight classes in professional boxing. McLeod's decision to compete in this weight class showcases his dedication and passion for the sport, as he willingly faces tough opponents in pursuit of greatness.
