Known by his fans and opponents alike as "King Kong," Ortiz has earned this nickname through his devastating punching power and ferocious fighting style. Standing at such an imposing height, it's no wonder that he strikes fear into the hearts of his adversaries.

Weighing in at a solid 108.86kg, Ortiz is a force to be reckoned with among the biggest and strongest fighters in the sport. The heavyweight division is known for its explosive power and knockout ability, and Ortiz fits right in with the best of them.

Ortiz's journey in boxing has been one filled with triumphs and setbacks. Like any great boxer, he has faced his fair share of challenges, both inside and outside of the ring. But through it all, Ortiz has shown resilience and determination, never letting the setbacks define him.

Throughout his career, Ortiz has faced some of the best fighters in the world, going toe-to-toe with the likes of Deontay Wilder and Alexander Povetkin. These fights have showcased his skill and bravery, as well as his ability to go the distance when necessary.

One of Ortiz's greatest strengths as a boxer is his ability to adapt to different opponents and styles. As Ortiz continues to make waves in the boxing world, there is no doubt that he will leave a lasting legacy. His combination of power, skill, and resilience have established him as one of the top fighters in the heavyweight division. And with his unwavering determination, there's no telling how far Ortiz can go in his boxing career.
