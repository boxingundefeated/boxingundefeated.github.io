Biography

Jack Doyle, also known as "The Gorgeous Gael," was born on August 31, 1913. He was a formidable heavyweight boxer, known for his impressive reach of 79 inches (201cm). With a weight of 118.84kg, Doyle competed in the heavyweight weight class, showcasing his strength and power in the ring.

His nickname, "The Gorgeous Gael," embodied both his Irish heritage and his charismatic presence in and out of the ring.
