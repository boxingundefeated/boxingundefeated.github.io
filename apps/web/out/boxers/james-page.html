Biography

James Page, known by his ring name "Mighty Quinn," was born on April 1, 1971. Standing at a height of 180cm and possessing a reach of 185cm, Page belongs to the welterweight weight class in boxing. Though Page's career spanned several decades, his influence in the boxing world remains strong.
