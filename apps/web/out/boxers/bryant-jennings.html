Biography

Bryant Jennings, also known as "By-By," was born on September 25, 1984. Standing at an impressive height of 191cm and possessing a reach of 213cm, Jennings is a force to be reckoned with in the heavyweight division. With a weight of 103kg, he showcases power and strength that are essential in the sport of boxing.

Jennings, an orthodox fighter, has made waves in the boxing world with his exceptional skills and determination. As one of the top-ranked heavyweights, he has faced numerous formidable opponents throughout his career, showcasing his resilience and unwavering spirit inside the ring.
