Biography

Eric Mitchell, also known by his nickname "Murder," is a legendary figure in the world of boxing. Born on August 25, 1969, this Middleweight boxer left a lasting impact on the sport with his remarkable skills and relentless fighting style.

Standing at an impressive height of 177cm, Mitchell possessed the physical attributes and technique to dominate his opponents in the ring. With an orthodox stance, he utilized his reach and power effectively, earning him a reputation as a formidable force to be reckoned with.

Throughout his career, Eric Mitchell showcased his talents against some of the toughest competitors in the Middleweight division. His fights were always eagerly anticipated by fans who knew they were in for a thrilling display of boxing excellence.

Murder's fighting style relied heavily on his ability to deliver devastating punches with precision and speed.
