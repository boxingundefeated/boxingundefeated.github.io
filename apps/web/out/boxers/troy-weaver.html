Introducing Troy Weaver, a rising star in the world of boxing. Standing at an impressive height of 188cm with a reach that matches, Weaver possesses all the physical attributes of a formidable fighter. Known for his orthodox stance, Weaver combines technical skill with raw power to dominate in the ring.

Despite his relatively short professional career, Weaver has already made waves in the boxing scene.
