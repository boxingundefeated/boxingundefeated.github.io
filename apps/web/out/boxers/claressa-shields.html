Biography

When it comes to women's boxing, Claressa Shields has undoubtedly established herself as one of the sport's all-time greats. Born on March 17, 1995, Shields has risen to prominence through her remarkable skills and achievements both inside and outside the ring. Standing at a height of 173cm and weighing in at 74.84kg, she competes in the middleweight and super middleweight weight classes.

A nickname that resonates deeply with Shields is "GWOAT," which stands for "Greatest Woman of All Time." This moniker speaks volumes about the impact she has had in the world of boxing and her ongoing pursuit to leave a lasting legacy in the sport.

Shields' boxing journey began at a young age, and it didn't take long for her talent to become evident. As an amateur, she quickly became a force to be reckoned with, securing numerous accolades and titles. In fact, she became the first American woman to ever win an Olympic gold medal in boxing, triumphing in both the 2012 London Games and the 2016 Rio de Janeiro Games.

Shields' rise to fame continued as she transitioned to the professional ranks. With her exceptional skills and determination, she quickly made a name for herself. Shields has accomplished something unprecedented in the world of boxing â€“ becoming a two-time undisputed champion in just her 10th professional fight.

Her first undisputed title came in 2018 when she defeated Hanna Gabriels to capture the WBA, WBC, IBF, and WBO middleweight belts. Shields' remarkable achievement made her the first woman in boxing history to hold all four major titles in any weight division simultaneously. This feat solidified her position as a true trailblazer and a dominant force in women's boxing.

Not content with just one undisputed championship, Shields sought to make history once again. In 2019, she moved up to the super middleweight division and faced off against Christina Hammer. Shields defeated Hammer convincingly, securing the WBC, WBA, IBF, and WBO super middleweight titles, once again becoming the undisputed champion in an entirely new weight class.

Shields' success can be attributed to her exceptional skills in the ring. Known for her technical prowess, defensive prowess, and relentless aggression, she consistently puts on impressive performances. Her punches are delivered with precision and power, often overwhelming her opponents. Shields' ability to seamlessly transition between offense and defense makes her a formidable opponent for anyone she faces.

Outside of her boxing career, Shields has also made a significant impact as an advocate for women's rights and equality. Using her platform as a renowned athlete, she has spoken out against gender inequality in sports and has used her success to inspire and empower young girls around the world.

Looking ahead, there is no doubt that Shields will continue to make waves in the sport of boxing. Her determination, resilience, and exceptional talent make her a force to be reckoned with. Whether it's defending her titles or taking on new challenges, Shields consistently proves that she is far more than just a skilled boxer.

As one of the greatest female boxers to ever step foot in the ring, Claressa Shields has left an indelible mark on the sport. Her accomplishments speak for themselves, and her journey is far from over. With each fight, she continues to solidify her place in boxing history, inspiring a new generation of athletes along the way.
