Biography

Alex Leapai, born on October 16, 1979, is a force to be reckoned with in the heavyweight boxing division. Standing at an impressive height of 183cm and boasting a reach of 190cm, Leapai's physical attributes give him a significant advantage in the ring. With an orthodox stance and weighing in at 115.35kg, he is a true powerhouse in the world of professional boxing.

Although Leapai does not have any official nicknames, his reputation in the boxing community speaks for itself. With over 20 years of experience in the sport, he has carved out a name for himself as a formidable opponent who leaves a lasting impact on his opponents.

Leapai's journey in the sport of boxing has been nothing short of inspiring.
