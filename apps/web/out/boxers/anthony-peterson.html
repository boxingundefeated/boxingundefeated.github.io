Biography

Anthony Peterson, also known by his nickname "Hazardous," is a formidable boxer in the Lightweight division. Born on March 16, 1985, Peterson has dedicated his life to the sweet science of boxing, honing his skills and becoming a force to be reckoned with in the ring.

Standing at 173cm tall and boasting a reach of 188cm, Peterson possesses physical attributes that give him an advantage over his opponents. His orthodox stance allows him to utilize his reach effectively and deliver powerful punches with precision.

Competing in the Lightweight weight class, Peterson has faced some of the toughest opponents in the division. This weight class, typically ranging from 130 to 135 pounds, is known for its speed, agility, and technical skills. Peterson has proven time and again that he possesses all these qualities.
