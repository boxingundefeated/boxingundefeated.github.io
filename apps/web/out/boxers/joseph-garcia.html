Joseph Garcia, standing at a height of 170.18cm, is an orthodox featherweight boxer who has made a name for himself in the world of boxing. From a young age, Garcia showed a natural talent for boxing. His dedication and hard work helped him rise through the ranks, and soon he was competing in amateur bouts. It was during this time that he developed his solid orthodox stance, which has become a trademark of his style in the ring.

Garcia's decision to compete in the featherweight weight class was a strategic one. Weighing in at around 126 pounds, he found himself comfortable in this division and believed that it would give him the best chance of success. And he was right.

Throughout his career, Garcia has competed against some of the top names in the featherweight category. His perseverance and determination have earned him a respected place among his peers. Despite not having any notable nicknames, his opponents know better than to underestimate him.
