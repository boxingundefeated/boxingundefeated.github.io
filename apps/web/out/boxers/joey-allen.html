Throughout his career, Allen faced some of the toughest opponents of his era. From bruising battles with seasoned veterans to dominant victories against rising stars, he consistently proved himself to be a formidable adversary. Even after retiring from professional boxing, Allen remained involved in the sport as a mentor and coach. He shared his wealth of knowledge and experience with young fighters, helping to shape the next generation of boxing champions.

Today, Joey Allen's name continues to be synonymous with excellence in the boxing world. His impact on the sport cannot be overstated, and his name will forever be etched in the annals of boxing history.
