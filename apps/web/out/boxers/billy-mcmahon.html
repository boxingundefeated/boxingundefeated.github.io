When it comes to the world of boxing, few names carry as much weight as Billy McMahon. Born on November 25, 1924, McMahon has left an indelible mark on the sport that still resonates with fans today. Known for his incredible skill, relentless determination, and iconic orthodox stance, McMahon established himself as one of the best lightweight boxers of his era.

Outside of the ring, McMahon's impact on the sport of boxing cannot be understated. His bouts drew unprecedented crowds, captivated audiences, and left a lasting impression on the boxing community.
