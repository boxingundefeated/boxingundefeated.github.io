Biography

Elvis "Dominican Kid" Rodriguez is a rising star in the world of professional boxing. Born on January 14, 1996, in the Dominican Republic, Rodriguez has quickly made a name for himself with his impressive skills and relentless determination inside the ring.

Standing at a height of 175 centimeters and boasting a reach of 180 centimeters, Rodriguez possesses the physical attributes that make him a formidable opponent in the super lightweight division. As a southpaw, he uses his unorthodox stance to his advantage, often catching opponents off guard with his slick footwork and powerful punches.

At a weight of 63.5 kilograms, Rodriguez is known for his speed and agility, allowing him to dart in and out of range while delivering devastating combinations. His ability to seamlessly transition between offense and defense has earned him the respect of his peers and the admiration of boxing fans worldwide.

With a nickname like the "Dominican Kid," Rodriguez carries the pride and expectations of his home country with him every time he steps into the ring. The Dominican Republic has a rich boxing history, producing world-class fighters such as Felix "Tito" Trinidad and Juan Guzman.
