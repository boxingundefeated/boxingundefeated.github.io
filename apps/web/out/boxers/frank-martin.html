Biography

Frank Martin, also known as "[]" in the boxing world, was born on January 21, 1995. Standing at a height of 173cm and weighing in at 135kg, Martin competes in the Lightweight weight class. Martin's proficiency in this stance gives him an edge in the Lightweight division.
