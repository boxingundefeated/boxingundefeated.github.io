Biography

William Zepeda Segura, better known as "El Camaron" or simply "Camaron," is an up-and-coming professional boxer who has been making waves in the lightweight division. Born on June 4, 1996, Zepeda is a young athlete with immense potential and a bright future ahead of him.

Standing at a height of 175cm, Zepeda possesses a solid reach of 177cm, which gives him an advantage in the ring. He is a southpaw, meaning he leads with his right hand and has a left-handed stance. This unorthodox style often catches opponents off guard and allows him to use angles effectively.

At 24 years old, Zepeda has already made a name for himself in the boxing world. He has been honing his skills for years, dedicating countless hours to perfecting his technique and strategy. As a lightweight, he competes in the 135-pound weight class, where he faces off against formidable opponents who push him to his limits.

One of Zepeda's greatest strengths is his speed and agility inside the ring.
