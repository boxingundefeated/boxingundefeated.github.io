Biography

Boxing fans have long been captivated by the power and skill of Mason Menard, the talented fighter known by his nickname "Rock Hard Mighty." Born on October 22, 1988, Menard has established himself as a force to be reckoned with in the boxing world. Standing at 168cm tall with a reach of 170cm, Menard possesses the physical attributes that make him a formidable opponent in the ring.

With an orthodox stance, Menard showcases his technical prowess and ability to generate power from his punches. His boxing style allows him to effectively utilize his reach and deliver knockout blows to his opponents. Menard's dedication to honing his skills and unwavering determination have catapulted him to great heights in his boxing career, earning him a loyal fan base and respect in the sport.

Menard's journey in the world of boxing has been one of both triumph and adversity. Throughout his career, he has faced various challenges, both inside and outside the ring. However, Menard's resilience and unwavering commitment to his craft have allowed him to overcome these obstacles and emerge as a true contender.

One of the defining moments in Menard's career came in April 2016 when he squared off against Eudy Bernardo. The fight ended in a stunning fashion as Menard landed a devastating right hook that sent Bernardo crashing to the canvas. The explosive knockout victory propelled Menard into the spotlight and solidified his reputation as a knockout artist.

Menard's knockout power and ability to end fights in spectacular fashion have earned him numerous accolades and recognition within the boxing community. His fans eagerly anticipate each bout, eager to witness the sheer force and skill he brings to the ring.
