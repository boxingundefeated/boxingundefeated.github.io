However, Ellis' reign as heavyweight champion was short-lived. He lost the title in his first defense against the legendary Joe Frazier. Jimmy Ellis may not have achieved the same level of fame as some of his contemporaries, but his contributions to the sport of boxing should not be overlooked. Unfortunately, Jimmy Ellis passed away on May 6, 2014, leaving behind a legacy that will forever be remembered in the annals of boxing history. His contributions to the sport and the impact he made on those who watched him compete will not be forgotten.
