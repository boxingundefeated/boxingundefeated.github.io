Biography

Eddie Machen was a heavyweight boxer born on June 15, 1932. Standing at an impressive height of 183cm, he possessed a reach of 75.2 inches (191cm). With an orthodox stance and belonging to the heavyweight weight class, Machen left a mark on the world of boxing during his career.

Machen, known for his fast footwork and powerful punches, was a force to be reckoned with in the ring. Despite not having any notable nicknames, his skills and achievements spoke for themselves.
