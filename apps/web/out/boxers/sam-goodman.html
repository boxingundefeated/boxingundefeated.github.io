Sam "Mad Bunch" Goodman is a rising star in the world of boxing. With his impressive skills and determination, he has been making a name for himself in the super bantamweight division. Born on October 10, 1998, Goodman is just 23 years old, but his talent and dedication have already earned him a place among the top contenders in his weight class.
