Biography

Donnie "Ahas" Nietes is a Filipino professional boxer who was born on May 12, 1982. Standing at a height of 160cm and boasting a reach of 165cm, Nietes has made a name for himself in the boxing world, competing in the Minimumweight and Super Flyweight weight classes.
