Biography

It's time to step into the ring and meet the formidable boxer, Freddy Lussick! Born on August 28, 2000, Lussick is a rising star in the boxing world with a bright future ahead of him. Standing at an impressive height of 180cm and weighing in at 84kg, Lussick possesses the physical attributes of a true powerhouse in the ring.

Lussick comes from a long line of talented boxers, and he is determined to make his mark in the sport. Although he may not have any flashy nicknames just yet, his skills and determination speak for themselves. With each fight, Lussick showcases his incredible talent and leaves his opponents trembling in their boots.

One of the key factors that sets Lussick apart from his competition is his orthodox stance. This means that his left hand is positioned in front and his right hand is positioned in the rear. This stance allows him to maintain a strong defensive posture while simultaneously unleashing powerful punches from his right hand.

When Lussick steps into the ring, spectators are in for an action-packed showdown. His explosive bursts of energy and lightning-fast punches keep audiences on the edge of their seats. Every movement is calculated, every punch precisely thrown with the intention to dominate his opponents.

Despite his young age, Lussick has already gained valuable experience in the boxing world. He has been training intensively for years, honing his skills and perfecting his technique. His dedication and commitment to his craft have paid off, as he continues to rise through the ranks and leave his mark on the sport.

It's not just Lussick's physical prowess that impresses fans and fellow boxers alike. His mental strength and resilience are equally admirable. As Lussick continues his boxing journey, it's clear that he has a bright future ahead of him.
