Jorge Vasquez, also known as "The Thriller," has been making waves in the boxing world for quite some time now. With an impressive record of wins, this bantamweight fighter has proven himself time and again to be a top contender in his weight class. But what sets him apart from the rest? Let's find out!

Vasquez's orthodox stance gives him a solid foundation and allows him to deliver powerful punches with precision. He utilizes his height advantage to dictate the range and control the pace of the fight. This tactical approach, along with his lightning-fast reflexes, makes him a formidable opponent in the ring.
