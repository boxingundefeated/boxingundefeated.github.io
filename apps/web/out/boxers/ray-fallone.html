While Fallone is still a rising prospect, his potential has not gone unnoticed by the professional boxing world. Promoters, trainers, and fellow fighters have all recognized his talent and have eagerly awaited his transition from the amateur ranks to the professional stage.

As Fallone embarks on his professional boxing career, he will face tougher opponents and higher stakes.
