Biography

Neeraj Goyat, also known as "Gangster," is a professional boxer hailing from India. Born on November 11, 1991, Goyat has carved a name for himself in the world of boxing with his exceptional skills and tenacity inside the ring.

Standing at a height of 171cm with a reach of 171cm, Goyat possesses excellent physical attributes that help him excel in his weight class. Competing in the bantamweight division, Goyat weighs in at 61.2kg, making him a formidable opponent for anyone who steps in the ring with him.

Goyat's fighting style is orthodox, meaning he primarily uses his right hand as his power punch. With this stance, Goyat utilizes his quick footwork and swift movements to outmaneuver his opponents and land powerful blows.

Throughout his career, Goyat has faced numerous challenges and showcased his ability to overcome them. His perseverance and unwavering determination have helped him achieve remarkable success in the ring, making him one of the most respected boxers in India.

While Neeraj Goyat may not be a household name worldwide, his accomplishments in the boxing arena speak for themselves. Goyat has participated in several high-profile bouts and has proven himself to be a force to be reckoned with.
