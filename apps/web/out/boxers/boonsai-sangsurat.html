Biography

Boonsai Sangsurat, also known as "Street King" Tohchoodee, is a skilled boxer whose career has spanned multiple weight classes and earned him a respected reputation in the world of boxing. Born on July 12, 1978, Sangsurat has consistently showcased his talents and dedication throughout his career, making him a force to be reckoned with in the ring.

Standing at a height of 163cm, Sangsurat possesses a compact frame that allows him to excel in lower weight classes, such as Minimumweight, Flyweight, Super Flyweight, Bantamweight, and Super Bantamweight. His agility and speed are essential assets in these divisions, enabling him to outmaneuver his opponents and deliver precise punches.

Sangsurat's orthodox stance further enhances his performance in the ring. By positioning his left foot forward and his right foot back, he gains stability and balance during his fights. This stance enables him to generate power in his punches while maintaining impeccable defensive maneuverability.

Throughout his career, Sangsurat has displayed his versatility by competing in various weight classes. In addition to the aforementioned divisions, he has also tested his skills in Featherweight, Super Featherweight, Lightweight, Super Lightweight, Welterweight, Super Welterweight, Middleweight, and Super Middleweight. This adaptability showcases Sangsurat's determination and willingness to challenge himself against opponents of different sizes and styles.

One of the key aspects that sets Sangsurat apart from his peers is his ability to adapt his fighting style according to his opponents. Whether he is facing a taller, more aggressive opponent or a shorter, more defensive one, Sangsurat can adjust his strategy to exploit their weaknesses and capitalize on his own strengths.

Interestingly, the nickname "Street King" Tohchoodee adds a vibrant touch to Sangsurat's persona. It reflects his skill and tenacity inside the ring, as well as his unyielding mentality. This nickname resonates with fans and adds to his appeal as a boxer.

Sangsurat has demonstrated an impressive level of consistency and resilience throughout his career, solidifying his place among the boxing elite. His experience in multiple weight classes, combined with his natural talent and adaptability, have made him a formidable opponent for anyone who steps into the ring with him.

As boxing fans, we eagerly anticipate Sangsurat's future fights and continue to witness his growth as a boxer. His unwavering determination and ability to overcome challenges make him a true champion in our eyes. It is this unwavering spirit that has brought him immense success throughout his career and will undoubtedly continue to do so in the future.
