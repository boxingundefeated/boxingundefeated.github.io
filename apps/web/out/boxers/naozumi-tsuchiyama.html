Biography

Naozumi Tsuchiyama, also known as "Gensan," is a professional boxer who was born on July 26, 1982. With a career spanning over two decades, Tsuchiyama has established himself as a formidable force in the world of boxing.

Tsuchiyama, hailing from Japan, has earned his place among the boxing elite through sheer dedication and hard work. His nickname, "Gensan," is testament to his fighting spirit and his ability to overcome obstacles in and out of the ring.

As a boxer, Tsuchiyama has predominantly fought using the orthodox stance, a traditional style that favors his right hand as his power punch. This stance allows him to generate maximum force behind his punches, often catching opponents off guard with his explosive combinations.

Throughout his illustrious career, Tsuchiyama has faced numerous formidable opponents, proving his mettle in the ring time and time again. His journey in the sport has been marked by immense triumphs as well as daunting challenges, showcasing his resilience as a fighter.
