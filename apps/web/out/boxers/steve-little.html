Biography

Steve Little, a former professional boxer in the Super Middleweight division, is known for his impressive skills and determination inside the ring. Born on June 9, 1965, Little made his mark in the world of boxing with his orthodox stance and relentless pursuit of victory.

Standing at a height of 178cm, Little possessed the physical attributes necessary for success in the Super Middleweight division. With a reach of 77 inches (196 cm), he had the ability to keep opponents at bay while delivering powerful strikes.

Throughout his career, Steve Little showcased his technical abilities and proved to be a force to be reckoned with. His orthodox stance allowed him to effectively utilize his power punches and counter his opponents' attacks with precision.

Competing in the Super Middleweight weight class, Little faced some of the toughest opponents in the division. His determination and unwavering commitment to the sport earned him recognition as a formidable contender.

Over the years, Little became known for his relentless work ethic and dedication to his craft. He consistently pushed himself to the limits, both in and out of the gym, in order to refine his skills and stay at the top of his game.

One of the key factors that set Steve Little apart was his ability to adapt to different fighting styles. He was a versatile boxer who could adjust his strategy based on the strengths and weaknesses of his opponents. This adaptability allowed him to overcome challenging situations and emerge victorious in the ring.

Throughout his career, Steve Little's fans marveled at his ability to deliver powerful punches with tremendous speed and accuracy.
