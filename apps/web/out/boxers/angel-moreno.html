Biography

Angel Moreno, also known as "El Moreno," is a professional boxer who was born on November 14, 1983. With a height of approximately 162.56cm, he competes in the welterweight weight class. Moreno is known for his orthodox stance in the ring, and his relentless style has made him a force to be reckoned with.

Moreno's journey in the world of boxing began at a young age. Growing up, he had a natural talent for the sport and quickly fell in love with the exhilaration of stepping into the ring. Over the years, he honed his skills, dedicating countless hours to training and perfecting his technique.

As an orthodox fighter, Moreno relies on his strong right hand as his power punch. His stance allows him to generate a significant amount of force with his punches, making him a formidable opponent to anyone who steps into the ring with him. His ability to deliver powerful blows has earned him respect and recognition among boxing enthusiasts.

One of the key factors that sets Moreno apart from many other boxers is his tenacity. He is known for his unwavering determination and relentless pursuit of victory.
