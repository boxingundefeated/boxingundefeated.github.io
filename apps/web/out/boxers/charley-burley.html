Charley Burley, also known by his nickname "Deacon Burley," was born on September 6, 1917. He was a skilled boxer who competed in the welterweight and middleweight weight classes during his career. Standing at a height of 175cm and with a reach of 191cm, Burley possessed the physical attributes that aided him in the ring. His orthodox stance allowed him to utilize his technical skills to their full potential.

Despite his undeniable talent, Burley never received a world title shot, an injustice that many boxing fans and historians still lament today.
