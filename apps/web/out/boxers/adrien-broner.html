Biography

Adrien Broner, also known as "The Problem," is a professional boxer who was born on July 28, 1989. With a height of 168cm and a reach of 175cm, Broner belongs to the orthodox stance. He has competed in various weight classes throughout his career, including Super Lightweight, Welterweight, and Super Welterweight.
