Biography

Danny O'Connor, also known as "Danny O" or "Danny Bhoy," is a professional boxer who was born on March 30, 1985. Standing at a height of 175cm and weighing in at 70.31kg, O'Connor is a true force to be reckoned with in the ring.

One of the most fascinating aspects of O'Connor's boxing style is his stance. He is a southpaw, which means that he leads with his right hand and has his right foot forward, opposite to the conventional orthodox stance. This gives him a unique advantage when facing opponents who are more accustomed to fighting orthodox boxers.

With a reach of 171cm, O'Connor has the ability to keep his opponents at bay while delivering powerful strikes. His long reach allows him to effectively jab and create openings for devastating combinations.
