Biography

Maxie Rosenbloom, also known as "Slapsie Maxie," was a prominent figure in the world of boxing. Born on November 6, 1906, he had an impressive career that left a lasting impact on the sport. Standing at a height of 179cm with a reach of 182cm, Rosenbloom was known for his unusual fighting style and unconventional approach in the ring. Rosenbloom started his professional boxing career in 1923 at the age of 17, quickly establishing himself as a force to be reckoned with. He competed in the light heavyweight weight class, where he excelled and showcased his exceptional skills.

Throughout his career, Rosenbloom demonstrated an impressive record and an uncanny ability to adapt to different opponents. He possessed an exceptional boxing IQ, which enabled him to study his adversaries' weaknesses and exploit them to his advantage.
