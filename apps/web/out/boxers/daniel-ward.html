Biography

When it comes to the world of boxing, there are few fighters who can truly leave a lasting legacy. Daniel Ward is one such fighter. Born on June 3, 1963, Ward has made a name for himself in the boxing world with his incredible talent and dedication to the sport. Throughout his career, he has competed in various weight classes, including Minimumweight, Light Flyweight, Flyweight, Super Flyweight, and Bantamweight. Ward's ability to adapt and excel in different weight divisions is a testament to his skill as a boxer.

Ward is known for his orthodox stance, which is commonly used in boxing. This stance involves the fighter placing their left foot forward and their right foot back, with their left hand and shoulder facing the opponent. This stance allows for strong punches and the ability to move quickly and efficiently around the ring.

Throughout his career, Ward has faced off against some of the best fighters in the world. His skills and determination have earned him a reputation as a formidable opponent, and he has proven time and time again that he has what it takes to come out on top.
