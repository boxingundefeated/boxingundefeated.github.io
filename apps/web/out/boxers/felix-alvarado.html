Biography

One of the rising stars in the world of boxing is Felix Alvarado, also known as "El Gemelo." Born on February 15, 1989, Alvarado has been making waves in the sport with his impressive skills and determination. Standing at a height of 163cm and boasting a reach of 170cm, he competes in the Light Flyweight weight class. Let's take a closer look at this talented boxer and what sets him apart from the rest.

From a young age, it was clear that Alvarado had a natural talent for boxing. He honed his skills and dedicated himself to the sport, resulting in a successful amateur career before turning professional. Alvarado's orthodox stance and impeccable technique make him a formidable opponent in the ring.
