Biography

Jack Bodell, also known as "The Fighting Fireman," is a legendary figure in the world of boxing. Born on September 11, 1940, Bodell hails from the United Kingdom and made a significant impact on the sport during his career, specifically in the Light Heavyweight division. Standing at an impressive height of 185cm, Bodell dominated his opponents with his commanding presence and remarkable boxing skills.

With his southpaw stance, Bodell was able to catch his foes off guard and maintain a strong defense, making it difficult for them to launch effective attacks.
