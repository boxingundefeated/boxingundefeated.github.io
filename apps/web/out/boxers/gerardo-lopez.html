Gerardo Lopez's orthodox stance is a classic approach in boxing, where the boxer's left hand and foot are forward, while the right hand and foot are positioned back. This stance allows for a strong lead jab and powerful right-hand punches. It is a traditional stance that most boxers are trained in and offers a solid foundation for offensive and defensive strategies.
