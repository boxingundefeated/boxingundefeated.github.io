Abel Ramos, born on July 2, 1991, is a professional boxer who competes in the welterweight division. Standing at a height of 175cm, Ramos possesses a reach of 175cm and fights in the orthodox stance. With a weight of 66.22kg, he is a formidable force in his weight class.
