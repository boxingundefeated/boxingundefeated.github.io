Biography

Are you ready for an electrifying display of skill and power inside the boxing ring? Look no further than Defry Palulu, a formidable force in the super featherweight division. With an impressive record and a unique fighting style, Palulu has captivated fans with his remarkable performances. In this article, we will take a closer look at the man behind the gloves and delve into his boxing journey.

Born on December 24, 1990, Palulu has always had a passion for combat sports. Standing at 173cm tall with a reach of 185cm, he possesses the physical attributes necessary to excel in the ring. But what truly sets him apart is his southpaw stance, a style that often surprises opponents and presents a challenge they must overcome.

Palulu competes in the super featherweight division, where his weight is meticulously maintained at 58.9kg. A crucial aspect of any boxer's training regimen is attaining and sustaining the appropriate weight class. Palulu's dedication to maintaining his weight ensures that he is always in peak physical condition, ready to take on any opponent that steps in his path.
