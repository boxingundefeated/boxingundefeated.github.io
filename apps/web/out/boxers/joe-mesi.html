Biography

Joe Mesi, also known as "Baby Joe," is a professional heavyweight boxer who was born on November 27, 1973. Standing at an impressive height of 185cm and boasting a reach of 201cm, Mesi is a force to be reckoned with inside the boxing ring.

With his orthodox stance, Mesi utilizes a traditional boxing style that focuses on a strong jab, powerful hooks, and devastating uppercuts. His reach advantage gives him the ability to keep his opponents at a distance while unleashing his signature punches.
