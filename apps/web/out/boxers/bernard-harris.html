Biography

Bernard Harris is a phenomenal boxer who has made a name for himself in the world of professional boxing. Born on March 10, 1974, Harris has enjoyed a successful career spanning multiple weight classes. Standing at a height of 165cm, with a reach of 178cm, he has proven to be a force to be reckoned with in the ring.
