Biography

Austin Trout, also known as "No Doubt," is a professional boxer hailing from the United States. Born on September 18, 1985, Trout has made a name for himself in the sport with his impressive skills inside the ring.

Standing at 177cm tall with a reach of 183cm, Trout competes in the super welterweight division. His southpaw stance gives him a unique advantage and makes him a formidable opponent for anyone who steps into the ring with him.

Trout's boxing career has spanned over two decades, and he has faced some of the toughest opponents in the sport. His dedication and hard work have earned him a reputation as a skilled and disciplined fighter.

One of the things that sets Trout apart from other boxers is his signature nickname, "No Doubt." This nickname perfectly captures his confident and unwavering belief in his abilities as a boxer. When Trout steps into the ring, there is no doubt that he will give his all and leave everything on the line.

Trout's journey to becoming a professional boxer started at a young age. He had a deep passion for the sport and dedicated countless hours to training and honing his skills. His hard work paid off, and he made his professional debut in 2005.

Since then, Trout has amassed an impressive record, with notable wins against top contenders in the super welterweight division. His skillful footwork, lightning-fast punches, and ability to adapt to different fighting styles have made him a force to be reckoned with.

Trout's fighting style is a combination of finesse and power. He has the ability to outsmart his opponents with his quick movements and counterpunching skills, while also delivering devastating blows that can end a fight in an instant.

Outside the ring, Trout is known for his humility and sportsmanship. Despite his success, he remains grounded and is always respectful towards his opponents. This has earned him the admiration and respect of both fans and fellow boxers.
