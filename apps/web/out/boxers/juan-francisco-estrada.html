Biography

Juan Francisco Estrada, known by his nickname "Gallo," is a renowned Mexican professional boxer who has made waves in the boxing world. Born on April 14, 1990, Estrada has proven himself to be a force to be reckoned with in the super flyweight division.

Standing at a height of 163cm with a reach of 168cm, Estrada's compact frame allows him to utilize his agility and speed inside the ring. His orthodox stance has become his signature, allowing him to effectively strategize and execute his punches.

Estrada's journey as a professional boxer has been nothing short of remarkable. He has faced numerous top-ranked opponents and has emerged victorious in several high-stakes bouts. His unique style, combined with his determination and skill, has earned him a loyal fan base and the respect of his peers.

One of Estrada's most notable achievements came in 2018 when he defeated Srisaket Sor Rungvisai to claim the WBC super flyweight title. This victory showcased his ability to adapt and overcome challenges, solidifying his status as one of the best in his weight class.

Throughout his career, Estrada has consistently displayed his technical prowess and ring intelligence. He possesses a remarkable understanding of distance and timing, allowing him to land precise and powerful punches. His ability to seamlessly switch between offensive and defensive strategies keeps his opponents on their toes.

When it comes to Estrada's boxing style, he is known for his counterpunching skills. He has the ability to read his opponent's movements, allowing him to anticipate their punches and effectively counter with his own. This unique skill set has made him a formidable opponent in the ring.

Estrada's boxing journey has been a testament to his determination and perseverance. He has faced his fair share of setbacks and challenges but has always managed to bounce back stronger. From grueling training sessions to intense sparring matches, Estrada's work ethic and commitment to his craft are truly commendable.

Off the boxing ring, Estrada is known for his humility and respect for his opponents. He conducts himself with professionalism and sportsmanship, earning the admiration of both fans and fellow boxers. It is this combination of talent, character, and dedication that makes Estrada not only a formidable boxer but also a role model for aspiring athletes.

Estrada's next bout is eagerly anticipated by boxing enthusiasts worldwide. Fans are eager to see him in action once again and witness his exceptional skills unfold in the ring. As he continues to rise in prominence, it is clear that Estrada's influence in the boxing world will only continue to grow.
