When it comes to weight classes, Seniesa Estrada is a true force to be reckoned with. She has competed in the Minimumweight, Light Flyweight, and Flyweight divisions, showcasing her versatility and ability to adapt to different weight classes.

Nicknamed "Super Bad," Estrada lives up to her moniker every time she steps into the ring. Her aggressive fighting style combined with lightning-fast hand speed makes her a formidable opponent for anyone who dares to face her.

But what sets Seniesa Estrada apart from her peers is her incredible burstiness and perplexity. It's not just her physical attributes that make Seniesa Estrada a remarkable boxer, but also her mental toughness and unwavering determination. She has overcome numerous obstacles and setbacks throughout her career, demonstrating her resilience and refusal to give up.
