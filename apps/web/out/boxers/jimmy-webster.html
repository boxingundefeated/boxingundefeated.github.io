When it comes to legendary boxers, few names stand out like that of Jimmy Webster. Born on September 16, 1924, Webster made a lasting impact on the sport during his career. Although he may not have had any official nicknames, his dedication and skill in the ring set him apart from his peers.

Webster was a force to be reckoned with in the flyweight and bantamweight weight classes. Standing at a weight of 53.52kg, he demonstrated remarkable speed and agility in his fights. His ability to move swiftly around the ring and deliver lightning-fast punches made him a formidable opponent.

Throughout his career, Webster faced off against some of the toughest and most talented boxers of his time. He never shied away from a challenge and always brought his best to every fight. His determination and resilience were key factors in his success and earned him the respect of both fellow fighters and fans alike.

One of the most remarkable aspects of Webster's boxing skills was his ability to adapt to different weight classes. Moving between flyweight and bantamweight, he showcased his versatility as a fighter.
