Biography

Roberto Garcia, also known as "La Amenaza," is an accomplished boxer with an impressive career spanning over two decades. Standing at a height of 175cm and boasting a reach of 183cm, Garcia has used his physical attributes to his advantage inside the squared circle. Born and raised with a fighting spirit, this Mexican-born pugilist has left a lasting impact on the sport of boxing.

With a powerful orthodox stance, Garcia has exhibited tremendous skill and technique throughout his career. His ability to deliver devastating punches while maintaining his balance has made him a formidable opponent in the ring. Whether facing off against fellow orthodox fighters or southpaws, Garcia has proven his versatility and adaptability.

Over the years, Garcia has earned a reputation as a skilled tactician, often formulating comprehensive game plans to exploit his opponents' weaknesses. His ability to assess and exploit openings in his opponents' defenses has allowed him to secure victories in numerous high-profile bouts. Garcia's boxing acumen is a testament to his dedication and commitment to the sport.
