Biography

Tony Tucker, also known as "TNT," is an accomplished boxer in the light heavyweight weight class. Born on December 27, 1958, Tucker has left an indelible mark on the world of professional boxing with his explosive power and formidable skills. Throughout his career, Tucker has showcased his skills as a boxer with an orthodox stance. This means that he favors leading with his left hand and right foot, a popular approach that allows for strong punches and effective combinations. His proficiency in this stance has played a crucial role in his success as a light heavyweight.
