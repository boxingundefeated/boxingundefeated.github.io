Biography

Paul Bradshaw, born on September 26, 1967, is an esteemed professional boxer known for his orthodox stance and incredible skills inside the ring. With over 20 years of experience, he has firmly established himself as one of the most respected names in the sport.

Bradshaw's orthodox stance is a testament to his dedication to the fundamentals of boxing. This stance has served Bradshaw well, forming the foundation of his formidable punching arsenal.
