Biography

Sergey Kovalev, also known as "Krusher," is a formidable force in the boxing ring. Born on April 2, 1983, the Russian-born fighter has made a name for himself in the Light Heavyweight weight class. Standing at a height of 183cm with a reach of 184cm, Kovalev possesses the physical attributes that make him a formidable opponent for any challenger.

Known for his orthodox stance and aggressive style, Kovalev has built a reputation as a power puncher with devastating knockout power. His nickname, "Krusher," perfectly encapsulates his ability to overpower his opponents with his heavy hands. With a professional boxing record boasting numerous knockouts, Kovalev has proven time and time again that he possesses the punch that can end a fight at any moment.

Throughout his career, Kovalev has faced off against some of the toughest competitors in the light heavyweight division. His technical skills combined with his physical strength have allowed him to dominate his opponents and secure impressive victories.

Since then, Kovalev has shown immense resilience, working his way back to the top of the light heavyweight division. He has faced and defeated top contenders, reclaiming his position as one of the elite fighters in the weight class.
