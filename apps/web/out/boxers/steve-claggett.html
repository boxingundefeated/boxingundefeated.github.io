Biography

Steve Claggett, also known as "Dragon," is a talented professional boxer born on June 11, 1989. With a height of 173cm and a reach of 178cm, he possesses the physical attributes necessary to excel in the ring. Claggett is an orthodox fighter, meaning he primarily leads with his left hand and has his right hand and foot as his dominant side. He competes in the super lightweight and welterweight weight classes, showcasing his versatility and willingness to challenge himself against different opponents.
