Biography

Regis Prograis, also known as "Rougarou," is a rising star in the world of professional boxing. Standing at 173cm tall, with a reach of 170cm, Prograis competes in the super lightweight weight class, where he has captured the attention of boxing fans around the globe.

Born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana, Prograis' journey in the boxing world has been nothing short of remarkable. Overcoming personal struggles and adversity, he has emerged as one of the most exciting and talented fighters of his generation.

Prograis' nickname, "Rougarou," pays homage to his Louisiana roots. The term refers to a legendary werewolf-like creature known to inhabit the swamps and bayous of the state. Just like the ferocious Rougarou, Prograis possesses a relentless fighting style and an unwavering determination in the ring.

In his professional career, Prograis has amassed an impressive record of victories, showcasing his skills and power as a super lightweight. Bursting onto the scene with his explosive performances, he quickly gained a reputation as a fearsome puncher with devastating knockout power.

One of Prograis' most notable fights came in October 2018 when he faced Terry Flanagan for the vacant WBC Diamond super lightweight title. In a thrilling encounter, Prograis emerged victorious, displaying his exceptional speed, precision, and ring IQ.

Another highlight of Prograis' career was his participation in the World Boxing Super Series, an elite tournament featuring the best fighters in the super lightweight division. Prograis made his mark in the quarterfinals against Terry's brother Maurice Hooker, delivering an outstanding performance that ended in a knockout victory.

Throughout his career, Prograis has proven to be a versatile fighter, comfortable both in pressuring his opponents with relentless aggression and staying elusive while dictating the pace of the fight. His ability to adapt to different styles and strategies has made him a formidable opponent in the ring.

Prograis' boxing style consists of smooth footwork, fluid combinations, and knockout power in both hands. His punches come with speed and precision, often catching his opponents off guard. His ring presence is commanding, and he possesses a high boxing IQ, making strategic decisions that give him an advantage over his adversaries.

Outside of the ring, Prograis is known for his engaging personality and his willingness to connect with his fans. Through social media platforms, he provides insights into his training regimen, shares personal moments, and offers words of encouragement to his supporters.

When it comes to his training, Prograis leaves no stone unturned. He is known for his intense workouts and dedication to his craft. From grueling cardio sessions to honing his punching accuracy, he prioritizes every aspect of his training to ensure he is in peak physical condition.

Prograis' journey is a testament to the power of resilience and determination. Despite facing setbacks and obstacles, he has persevered and continued to chase his dreams. His success in the ring has inspired many aspiring boxers and has firmly established him as a force to be reckoned with.

As Prograis continues to climb the ranks of the super lightweight division, the boxing world eagerly anticipates his next move. With his skill set, power, and unwavering drive, it's clear that he has all the ingredients to become a world champion and leave an indelible mark on the sport of boxing.

Regis Prograis' journey is far from over, and there is no doubt that he will continue to captivate audiences with his extraordinary talent and thrilling performances inside the squared circle. Keep an eye on this rising star, for he is destined for greatness.
