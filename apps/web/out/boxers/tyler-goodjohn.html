There's a new tornado brewing in the boxing ring, and his name is Tyler Goodjohn. Born on May 17, 1991, this talented fighter is ready to take the welterweight division by storm. Standing at 175cm with a reach of 71in (180cm), Goodjohn possesses the physical attributes that make him a formidable opponent inside the squared circle.

Goodjohn's nickname, fittingly, is "Tornado," and it's easy to see why. When he steps into the ring, he unleashes a whirlwind of punches, displaying a relentless attacking style that keeps his opponents on their toes and in a constant state of defense. Whether it's his lightning-fast jabs or thunderous hooks, Goodjohn's punches come with powerful force, leaving his opponents dazed and confused.

Being an orthodox fighter, Goodjohn stands with his left hand and left foot forward, while his right hand and right foot provide stability and power. When Goodjohn combines his stance with his reach of 71 inches, it becomes clear why his opponents find it challenging to evade his attacks.
