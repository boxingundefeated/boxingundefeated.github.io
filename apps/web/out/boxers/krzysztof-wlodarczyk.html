Biography

Krzysztof Wlodarczyk, also known by the nickname "Diablo," is a professional boxer who was born on September 19, 1981. With a height of 185cm and a reach of 188cm, Wlodarczyk possesses the physical attributes that make him a formidable opponent in the ring. As an orthodox boxer, he relies on his dominant right hand to deliver powerful punches and outmaneuver his opponents.

Wlodarczyk competes in the cruiserweight weight class, which ranges from 200 to 220 pounds. This weight class suits him well, as he can maximize his strength and agility without compromising his endurance. Throughout his career, Wlodarczyk has proven to be a force to be reckoned with in this division.

Wlodarczyk's journey as a professional boxer began years ago, and he has amassed an impressive record along the way.
