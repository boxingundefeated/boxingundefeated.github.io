Biography

Victor Morales, also known as "El Tornado," is a rising star in the world of professional boxing. Born on January 11, 1998, Morales has already made a name for himself in the featherweight division. With his impressive height of 170cm and orthodox stance, Morales brings a unique set of skills to the ring.

From a young age, Morales showed a natural talent for boxing. He quickly gained a reputation for his lightning-fast punches and strong footwork, earning him the nickname "El Tornado." This moniker perfectly captures his explosive style and relentless energy in the ring.
