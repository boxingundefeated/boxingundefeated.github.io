Biography

Marco Antonio Rubio, also known as "El Veneno," is a Mexican professional boxer who has made a significant mark in the Middleweight division. Born on June 16, 1980, Rubio has established himself as a formidable force in the ring with his impressive skills and tenacity. Standing at a height of 178cm and boasting a reach of 178cm, Rubio possesses the physical attributes necessary to excel in his weight class.

One of Rubio's defining features as a boxer is his orthodox stance, which provides him with a solid foundation to launch his powerful punches. Through the years, he has showcased his ability to deliver devastating blows, leaving opponents reeling from his punches.

Marco Antonio Rubio's career in professional boxing spans over two decades, with numerous accolades and achievements under his belt. As a Middleweight fighter, he has faced some of the biggest names in the sport, consistently displaying his skill and determination in the ring.
