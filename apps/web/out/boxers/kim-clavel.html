Kim Clavel is a rising star in the world of boxing. Born on September 9, 1990, this Canadian boxer has already made a name for herself in the sport. In 2017, Clavel made her professional debut, and she has been making waves ever since. Her explosive style and ability to control the pace of a fight have earned her fans and endorsements alike. The boxing world has taken notice of this rising star, and her future looks bright.
