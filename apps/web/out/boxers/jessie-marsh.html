Biography

Jessie Marsh, born on October 16, 1979, is a highly skilled professional boxer in the light heavyweight weight class. With his orthodox stance and remarkable talent in the ring, he has made a name for himself as a force to be reckoned with in the boxing world.

Although Marsh doesn't have any known nicknames, his boxing prowess speaks for itself. He has built an impressive career and has garnered a strong fan base over the years.

When it comes to his fighting style, Marsh is known for his orthodox stance. This means that his left hand and left foot are positioned forward, allowing him to generate power from his right hand, which is his lead hand.

As a light heavyweight boxer, Marsh competes in one of the most exciting weight classes in the sport. The light heavyweight division has a weight limit of 175 pounds, and it is known for its explosive combination of speed and power.
