Biography

Boxer Noel Gevor Mikaelyan has taken the boxing world by storm with his impressive skills and determination in the ring. Born on March 9, 1992, in Yerevan, Armenia, Gevor has established himself as a force to be reckoned with in the cruiserweight division.

Standing at 6 feet 3 inches tall and weighing around 200 pounds, Gevor possesses the perfect physique for a cruiserweight boxer. His long reach and sturdy frame allow him to deliver powerful punches while maintaining good defensive skills.

Gevor's boxing journey began at a young age when he discovered his passion for the sport. He started training and honing his skills under the guidance of his coach, Hector Javier Ramirez. With Ramirez's guidance and Gevor's natural talent, it didn't take long for Gevor to make a name for himself in the amateur boxing scene.

At the age of 18, Gevor made his professional debut in 2010 and quickly made a statement by winning his first seven fights, all by knockout. His aggressive fighting style and his ability to finish fights early caught the attention of boxing fans and experts alike.

Throughout his professional career, Gevor has faced tough opponents and showcased his versatility and adaptability in the ring. He has proven himself in both offensive and defensive aspects of boxing, displaying excellent footwork, head movement, and counter-punching skills.

One of Gevor's most notable performances was his victory over fellow cruiserweight boxer Mirko Larghetti in 2014. The fight, which took place in Hamburg, Germany, went the full twelve rounds. Gevor's superior boxing skills and strategy earned him a unanimous decision win, solidifying his position as a top contender in the division.

Gevor's professional record currently stands at 24 wins, 1 loss, and 1 draw, with 10 victories coming by way of knockout. He has faced some of the best boxers in the cruiserweight division and has proven his ability to compete at a high level.

Aside from his success inside the ring, Gevor also prides himself on being a positive role model and giving back to his community. He regularly participates in charitable events and works to inspire the younger generation to follow their dreams.

Gevor's journey in boxing is far from over. With his dedication, determination, and unwavering work ethic, he has the potential to achieve even greater heights in his career. As he continues to train and improve his skills, boxing fans around the world eagerly await his next fight and the spectacle he brings to the ring.

In conclusion, Noel Gevor Mikaelyan is a highly talented and accomplished boxer. His skill, determination, and positive impact on and off the boxing ring make him a remarkable athlete and role model. As he continues to write his story in the world of boxing, there's no doubt that Gevor will continue to leave a lasting legacy in the sport.
