Biography

Edwin Rosario, also known by his nickname "Chapo," was born on March 15, 1963. Standing at a height of 168cm and with a reach of 169cm, Rosario made a name for himself in the world of boxing. With an orthodox stance and incredible skills, he competes in the lightweight, super lightweight, and welterweight weight classes. Let's take a closer look at the journey of this remarkable fighter.

From the early stages of his career, Rosario showed immense promise and talent. His natural athleticism and dedication to improving his skills allowed him to climb the ranks quickly. Rosario possesses a unique combination of power, speed, and precision, which made him a formidable opponent in the ring.

One of Rosario's most memorable fights took place on June 13, 1986, against fellow lightweight champion Jose Luis Ramirez. In this thrilling bout, Rosario showcased his exceptional boxing skills and knockout power. With relentless determination, he delivered a powerful left hook that sent Ramirez to the canvas in the fourth round, earning Rosario the victory and securing the WBC Lightweight title.

Rosario's success continued as he moved up in weight classes. He proved his versatility and adaptability by competing in the super lightweight and welterweight divisions, showcasing his ability to compete against bigger opponents without sacrificing his speed and agility.
