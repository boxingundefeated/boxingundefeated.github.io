Biography

When it comes to the world of boxing, one name that has been making waves in recent years is Jose Espinoza. Born on June 15, 1988, Espinoza has quickly risen through the ranks to establish himself as a formidable force in the middleweight division. With a height of 180cm and a reach of 186cm, he possesses the physical attributes necessary to compete at the highest level.

One aspect that sets Espinoza apart from his peers is his southpaw stance. As a left-handed fighter, he presents a unique challenge to opponents who are more accustomed to facing orthodox fighters. This advantage allows him to unleash powerful punches from unexpected angles, often catching his opponents off guard.
