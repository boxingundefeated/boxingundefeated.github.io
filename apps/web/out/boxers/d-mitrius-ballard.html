Biography

D'Mitrius "Big Meech" Ballard, born on March 15, 1993, is a professional boxer hailing from the USA. With a height of 183cm and a reach of 188cm, Ballard possesses the physical attributes that have allowed him to compete in the middleweight weight class.

With a weight of 72.57kg, Ballard has established himself as a formidable force in the ring. His fighting stance is orthodox, which means he leads with his left hand and right foot forward. This stance allows him to generate power and deliver devastating punches.

The nickname "Big Meech" has become synonymous with Ballard's boxing career. It symbolizes his imposing presence in the ring and serves as a reminder to his opponents of his ability to dominate the competition.

Ballard's journey in the world of boxing began at a young age, and he has honed his skills over the years to become a well-rounded fighter. He combines his physical attributes with technical prowess and a strategic approach to outsmart his opponents.

Throughout his career, Ballard has faced numerous challenges and has consistently displayed perseverance and determination. He has fought against some of the best fighters in his weight class, proving his mettle and earning the respect of his peers.

One of his greatest strengths is his versatility in the ring. Ballard can adapt his style to match the strengths and weaknesses of his opponents. Whether it's using his reach advantage to keep his opponents at bay or utilizing his power to deliver devastating hooks and uppercuts, he possesses a wide array of tools in his arsenal.

Ballard's ability to control the distance and pace of the fight is exceptional. He often dictates the tempo with his footwork and sets up his combinations with precision. His opponents find it challenging to find openings in his defense, as he possesses quick reflexes and excellent head movement.

When it comes to his offensive tactics, Ballard is a force to be reckoned with. His punches are precise and calculated, and he knows how to generate power behind each strike. Whether it's devastating body shots or lightning-fast hooks, his punches leave an impact.

Furthermore, his defensive skills are equally impressive. Ballard has a knack for slipping punches and evading his opponents' attacks. This defensive prowess allows him to minimize damage while creating opportunities to counter-attack.

As a middleweight boxer, Ballard competes in one of the most competitive weight classes in the sport. The middleweight division has a rich history, with legendary fighters like Sugar Ray Robinson and Marvin Hagler leaving their mark.

Being a part of this esteemed weight class pushes Ballard to continually improve and showcase his skills. It provides him with opportunities to face off against top-ranked opponents and climb the ladder towards a championship title.

With each fight, Ballard continues to grow as a boxer. He analyzes his performances, identifies areas for improvement, and works tirelessly to refine his craft. This dedication and commitment to his training have propelled him to new heights in his career.
