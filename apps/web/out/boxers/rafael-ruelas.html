Biography

Rafael Ruelas is a former professional boxer who was born on April 26, 1971. Standing at a height of 180cm with a reach of 180cm, Ruelas made his mark in the world of boxing in the featherweight, lightweight, and super lightweight weight classes. Known for his determination and skill, Ruelas left a lasting impact on the sport during his career.
