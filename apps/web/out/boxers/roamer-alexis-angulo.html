Biography

Roamer Alexis Angulo, born on March 25, 1984, is an Ecuadorian professional boxer who competes in the super middleweight division. Standing at an impressive 185cm tall with a reach of 191cm, Angulo possesses an advantage in both size and reach over many of his opponents. With his orthodox stance and power-packed punches, he has earned a reputation as a formidable force inside the ring.

Although not widely recognized by a nickname, Angulo's skills and achievements speak for themselves. These qualities, combined with his physical attributes, make him a force to be reckoned with in the super middleweight division.

Roamer Alexis Angulo has faced numerous opponents throughout his professional career, each presenting their own unique challenges. However, Angulo has consistently risen to the occasion, showcasing his skills and proving his worth as a top-ranked boxer.

Throughout his career, Angulo has adopted an orthodox stance, which is the most common stance in boxing.
