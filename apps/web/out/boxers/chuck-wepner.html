Biography

Chuck Wepner, fondly known as "The Bayonne Bleeder," was born on February 26, 1939. He may not be a household name like Muhammad Ali or Mike Tyson, but Wepner made his mark in the boxing world in the 1970s. What makes Wepner truly remarkable is his durability and persistence in the ring. His nickname, "The Bayonne Bleeder," reflects his ability to withstand punishment and continue fighting. Wepner was known for his toughness, refusing to back down even when faced with formidable opponents.
