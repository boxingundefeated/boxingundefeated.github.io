Biography

Juan Meza, also known by his nickname "Kid Meza," is a retired professional boxer who made his mark in the super bantamweight division. Born on March 18, 1956, Meza showcased his skills and determination throughout his career, leaving an indelible impact on the sport of boxing. Standing at a height of 170cm with a reach of 175cm, Meza possessed the physical attributes to excel in the ring.

Meza's orthodox stance allowed him to utilize his power and technique effectively, making him a formidable opponent for anyone who stepped into the ring with him. He fought in an era where boxing was held in high regard and produced some of the greatest fighters of all time.
