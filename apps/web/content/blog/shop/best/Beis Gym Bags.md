Depending on your preferred workout activities, you may require specific features in your gym bag:

- **Strength training:** Look for a bag with a separate compartment or pouch for gym gloves or straps.

- **Running or cycling:** Choose a bag with a secure, padded compartment for your smartphone or music player, as well as reflective details for visibility during early morning or late evening workouts.

- **Group classes:** Pick a bag with enough space to accommodate extra clothes, towels, and water bottles for post-workout recovery and hydration.
