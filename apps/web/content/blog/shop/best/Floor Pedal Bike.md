### What is a floor pedal bike?

A floor pedal bike, also known as a kick scooter or push bike, is a human-powered vehicle with a platform for standing, large front wheel, and smaller rear wheel. It is propelled by pushing off the ground with one foot while the other foot remains on the platform, similar to a skateboard but with added support and stability from the pedals and handlebars.

### How does a floor pedal bike work?

A floor pedal bike works by having the rider stand on the platform, placing one foot on the front pedal while pushing off the ground with the other foot. As the front pedal moves downwards, the rider transfers their weight onto it, propelling the bike forward. The rider then switches feet, pushing off with the other foot while the first foot rests on the platform. This process is repeated to maintain momentum and move forward.

### How do I maintain and store a floor pedal bike?

To maintain a floor pedal bike, regularly inspect the wheels and bearings for damage or wear. Lubricate the bearings as needed to ensure smooth operation. Store the bike in a dry, secure location when not in use to prevent damage from the elements or theft.

### Is a floor pedal bike safe for children?

Floor pedal bikes can be safe for children to use, but it is essential to ensure that they have adequate balance and strength to safely control the bike. Additionally, children should always wear appropriate safety gear, such as a helmet, [knee pads](https://best.serp.co/shop/knee-pads/)[pads](https://best.serp.co/shop/pads/), and elbow pads, when riding a floor pedal bike.
