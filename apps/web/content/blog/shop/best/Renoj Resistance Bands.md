### General Advice for Using Renoj Resistance Bands

To make the most out of your resistance bands, follow these tips:

- Warm Up: Always start with a warm-up to prepare your muscles for exercise.

- Maintain Proper Form: Focus on maintaining proper form during each exercise to prevent injury and maximize effectiveness.

- Choose the Right Exercises: Select exercises that target specific muscle groups and align with your fitness goals.

- Cool Down: Finish each workout with a cool-down to help your muscles recover and prevent post-exercise soreness.
