### What is a still water bottle?

A still water bottle is a container specifically designed to hold and transport water without added carbonation, such as soda or mineral water. These bottles help in staying hydrated and reducing the use of single-use plastic bottles.

### How do still water bottles keep water cool?

Still water bottles are designed with double-walled insulation, which helps maintain the temperature of the water for a prolonged period. Some bottles may also have vacuum insulation technology that keeps the water cold for up to 24 hours.

### What materials are used to make still water bottles?

Still water bottles are typically made from stainless steel or BPA-free plastic, which are both safe and durable materials for storing water. Some bottles may be made from glass or silicone, depending on the brand and model.

### How do I clean a still water bottle?

1. Rinse the bottle thoroughly with warm, soapy water.

2. Use a long-handled bottle brush to clean the inside of the bottle and ensure that the entire surface is reached.

3. Clean the cap and any detachable parts with warm, soapy water or place them in the dishwasher if the manufacturer recommends it.

4. Allow the bottle and its parts to air dry completely before reassembling and using it again.

### How often should I replace my still water bottle?

It depends on the quality and maintenance of the bottle. If the bottle is well-maintained and made from high-quality materials, it may last for several years. However, replacing the bottle every 1-2 years is recommended to ensure that the bottle remains in good condition and doesn't develop any cracks or leaks over time.

### How do I choose the right size still water bottle?

Consider your daily water intake, the capacity you need to stay hydrated, and the available space in your bag or car's cupholder. Generally, a 16-20 oz bottle is suitable for daily use, while 25-32 oz bottles are suitable for outdoor activities or long drives.

### What are the benefits of using a still water bottle over disposable plastic bottles?

- Reduces plastic waste and pollution

- Saves money in the long run

- Is more convenient and environmentally friendly

- Allows you to control the quality and taste of the water you drink

### Are still water bottles safe for children?

Yes, still water bottles are safe for children to use if they are made from safe materials and have leak-proof lids or caps. However, always supervise young children to prevent accidents and ensure proper use of the bottle.

### How can I prevent the growth of bacteria in my still water bottle?

Clean your bottle regularly and thoroughly, including the cap and any detachable parts.
