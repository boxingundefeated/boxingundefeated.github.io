## FAQ

### What is an Airdyne Bike?

An Airdyne Bike is a type of exercise bike that uses air resistance, courtesy of a large fan, to help you pedal. It provides a smooth, quiet, and effective workout.

### How does the Airdyne Bike provide resistance?

The Airdyne Bike uses air resistance, which is provided by a large fan. The harder you pedal, the more resistance you create. This allows you to customize your workout intensity based on your own fitness level and goals.

### What are the benefits of using an Airdyne Bike?

An Airdyne Bike offers a full-body workout, as it requires the engagement of your arms and legs. It also provides low-impact cardio, which can be beneficial for joint health. The customizable resistance helps you challenge yourself and progress in your fitness journey.

### How does the Airdyne Bike measure workout intensity?

The Airdyne Bike uses a metric called RPM (revolutions per minute) to measure workout intensity. RPM indicates how fast you are pedaling, and can be connected to a heart rate monitor to help you gauge the intensity of your workout.

### What kind of workout routine can be performed on an Airdyne Bike?

Airdyne Bikes are versatile and can be used for a variety of workout routines, such as high-intensity interval training (HIIT), endurance training, and steady-state cardio. The customizable resistance allows you to tailor your workouts to your specific goals and fitness level.
