### What is a 5-gallon water bottle?

A 5-gallon water bottle is a large container designed to hold and dispense water. These bottles are typically made of high-density polyethylene (HDPE) or polycarbonate and are used with water coolers or dispensers.

### Why should I choose a BPA-free water bottle?

Bisphenol A (BPA) is a chemical that has been linked to health concerns. BPA-free water bottles are made without this chemical, offering a safer option for storing and consuming water. Choosing a BPA-free bottle helps to reduce exposure to this potentially harmful substance.

### How often should I replace my 5-gallon water bottle?

There's no specific timeline for replacing a 5-gallon water bottle. However, it's generally recommended to replace the bottle every 1-2 years, depending on usage, cleaning, and storage conditions. Signs that you should replace your bottle include cloudiness, odors, or visible damage to the bottle.

### How do I refill and clean my 5-gallon water bottle?

1. Rinse the bottle thoroughly with clean water to remove any residual drink or debris.

2. Mix a solution of warm water and dish soap in the bottle, and shake it to create suds. Let it sit for a few minutes.

3. Use a soft-bristled brush to scrub the inside of the bottle, paying special attention to the bottom and corners where bacteria can accumulate.

4. Rinse the bottle with clean water, making sure to remove all soap residue.

5. Allow the bottle to air dry upside down or with the cap off to prevent mold growth and unpleasant odors.
