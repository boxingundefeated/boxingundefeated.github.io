### How do I clean a 50 oz water bottle?

To clean a 50 oz water bottle thoroughly, follow these steps: \* Empty the bottle and remove the lid.

- Rinse the bottle and lid with warm, soapy water, ensuring that you clean all crevices and corners.

- For more stubborn residues, create a mixture of baking soda and water (1 tablespoon baking soda per cup of water) to create a paste. Apply this paste to the affected areas and let it sit for about 15 minutes before scrubbing gently with a soft-bristled brush.

- Rinse the bottle and lid thoroughly with clean water, and let them air dry.

Remember to clean the bottle and lid periodically to maintain optimal freshness and hygiene.
