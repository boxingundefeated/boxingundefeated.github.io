### How can I clean my large water bottle with straws?

Cleaning your water bottle with straws properly is crucial for maintaining good hygiene. We recommend following these steps:

1. Rinse with warm soapy water and use a bottle brush to clean the inside of the bottle and the straw.

2. Remove the straw and wash separately using a dish brush or straw cleaner.

3. Once everything is clean, thoroughly rinse all parts under running water to remove any soap residue.

### What materials are large water bottles with straws typically made from?

These bottles are usually made from three common materials:

- Plastic: Durable and lightweight, but not necessarily the most eco-friendly option.

- Stainless steel: Insulated designs keep your drinks cold or hot for hours; may be heavier but environmentally friendly.

- Glass: Offers a transparent view of your drink content and is BPA-free, but can be heavier and more fragile.

### Are there any specific care instructions for large water bottles with straws?

Yes, proper care extends the life and functionality of your bottle. Keep the following in mind:

- Do not use harsh chemicals or abrasive scrubbers when cleaning, as these can damage your bottle's surface.
