### How do I clean a butterfly water bottle?

To clean a butterfly water bottle, follow these steps:

1. Disassemble the bottle and remove any parts or accessories

2. Rinse the inside of the bottle and remove any debris with warm water

3. Add a few drops of dish soap to the bottle and fill it with warm water

4. Shake or swirl the soapy water around inside the bottle to clean it thoroughly

5. Empty the water and rinse the bottle with warm water until all soap residue is removed

6. Dry the bottle with a clean towel or let it air dry upside down
