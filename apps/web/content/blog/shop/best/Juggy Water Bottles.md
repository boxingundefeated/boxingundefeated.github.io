### Considerations When Purchasing Juggy Water Bottles

- Capacity: Choose a bottle based on your intended usage and needs. Smaller capacities may be suitable for personal use, while larger ones are better for group events or activities.

- Material: Juggy water bottles are typically made from materials like plastic, stainless steel, or glass. Consider your desired balance between durability, weight, and eco-friendliness.

- Spouts and Lids: Ensure that the design fits your preferences and needs. Some bottles may have twist-on lids, while others feature push-pull spouts or flip tops.

- Handle and Accessories: Some juggy water bottles come with built-in handles for easy carrying, while others may have additional accessories like fruit infusers or cleaning brushes.

### General Advice for Juggy Water Bottle Care and Maintenance

1. Wash the bottle and its parts thoroughly with soap and water after each use.

2. Allow the inside of the bottle to completely dry before reassembling and storing to prevent mold and mildew growth.

3. Avoid exposing the bottle to extreme temperatures, especially when filled with liquid, as this can cause damage to the material and affect the taste of the water.

4. Regularly inspect and clean the spouts, lids, and handles to ensure optimal performance and hygiene.
