Peloton Resistance Bands come in different resistance levels, ranging from light to heavy. The light bands have a resistance of about 5-10 pounds, while heavy bands can provide up to 50 pounds of resistance. As you progress in your fitness journey, you can move to higher resistance bands.

Before using the bands, warm up properly. Position yourself on a stable surface to avoid slipping.
