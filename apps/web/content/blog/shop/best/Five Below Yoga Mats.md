- Grip: A good yoga mat should provide enough grip to prevent slipping, especially during sweaty sessions. It's ideal to find mats that have textured surfaces or anti-slip materials like PVC or rubber.

Before making your purchase, think about these factors:

- Usage: Consider how often you'll use the mat and for what types of yoga practices. If you engage in intense styles like Ashtanga or power yoga, opt for a thicker, more durable mat.
