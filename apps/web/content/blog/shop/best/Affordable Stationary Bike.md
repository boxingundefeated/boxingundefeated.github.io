### Features to Consider

When choosing an affordable stationary bike, there are several key features you should consider:

- Resistance levels: Look for a bike with adjustable resistance levels so you can challenge yourself as you progress.

- Comfort: Make sure the seat is comfortable and adjustable for your height. Look for padded handles and a sturdy frame for added stability.

- Display: A digital display can help you track your workout progress, including distance, speed, time, and calories burned.

### Important Considerations

Before purchasing an affordable stationary bike, keep the following in mind:

- Space: Ensure you have enough space in your home or workout area to accommodate the bike.

- Warranty: Check the warranty offered by the manufacturer, especially if you plan on using the bike frequently.

- Maintenance: Choose a bike that is easy to maintain and assemble, so you can keep it running smoothly over time.
