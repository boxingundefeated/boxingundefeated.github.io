### What is the difference between a magnetic resistance and tension bands resistance in exercise bikes?

Magnetic resistance uses magnets to create resistance, offering a quieter and smoother ride. Tension bands resistance uses bands that create tension as you pedal, providing a more challenging workout but potentially being noisier and requiring more frequent maintenance.

The weight capacity of small exercise bikes can vary depending on the model. Typically, they can support up to 250 pounds, but some high-end models may accommodate up to 300 pounds. Always check the weight capacity before purchasing to ensure your safety and comfort during workouts.
