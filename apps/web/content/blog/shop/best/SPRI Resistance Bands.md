### How do I choose the right resistance for me?

Choose a resistance level based on your current fitness level and workout goals. For beginners or those looking for a light workout, opt for lower resistance levels such as yellow or red. For more advanced users or those targeting specific muscle groups, go for higher resistance levels like green, blue, or black. Always start with a lower resistance and gradually progress to higher ones as your strength and endurance improve.

### How can I maintain my SPRI resistance bands?

To prolong the life of your resistance bands, store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid exposing them to extreme temperatures or sharp edges. Regularly inspect your bands for signs of wear and tear, such as cracks or tears, and replace them if necessary. Wipe down your bands with a damp cloth after each use to keep them clean and free from germs or debris.

### What exercises can I perform using SPRI resistance bands?

SPRI resistance bands can be used for various exercises targeting different muscle groups, including arm curls, shoulder presses, chest presses, squats, lunges, and leg curls.
