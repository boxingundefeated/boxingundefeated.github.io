A Manduka Yoga Block is a rectangular, solid foam block designed to assist and enhance yoga practice. It offers support, stability, and versatility during various poses and helps practitioners maintain proper alignment.

Manduka yoga blocks can be used in a variety of ways to modify poses or to simply offer extra support. They're commonly used to help with balance, posture, flexibility, and depth in poses. Blocks can also alleviate pressure on joints and support beginners or those with limited mobility.

### What sizes and colors are available in Manduka yoga blocks?

Manduka yoga blocks come in two main sizes: the standard 9" x 6" x 3" and the travel-friendly 6" x 6" x 3". They're available in various colors to suit your style and match your Manduka yoga mat.

### What is the material of Manduka yoga blocks?

### How do I clean my Manduka yoga block?

To clean your Manduka yoga block, use a damp cloth and mild soap or yoga mat cleaner. Gently wipe the surface and allow it to air dry before storing. Avoid using harsh chemicals or submerging the block in water, as this may damage the foam material.

### How long will a Manduka yoga block last?

With proper care and handling, a Manduka yoga block can last for years. The durable foam materials used in these blocks are designed to withstand regular use and provide consistent support during your practice.

### Is it safe to use Manduka yoga blocks for hot yoga or in a heated studio?

Yes, Manduka yoga blocks are suitable for use in hot yoga classes and heated studios. However, it's essential to ensure that the foam materials do not come into direct contact with the hot surface, as this may cause damage. Use a yoga towel or mat to provide a barrier between the block and the heated surface.

### Do I need to use yoga blocks if I'm an experienced practitioner?

While yoga blocks are primarily useful for beginners and practitioners with limited mobility, experienced practitioners can also benefit from incorporating blocks into their practice. Blocks can provide additional challenges and help deepen poses, improving overall flexibility and balance.

### What is the difference between Manduka's cork and foam yoga blocks?

- **Cork Block:** Made from high-quality, sustainably-sourced Portuguese cork, this block offers a slightly denser and more stable surface compared to foam blocks. Cork yoga blocks are ideal for those looking for a more eco-friendly option or those who prefer a firm surface for support.

- **Foam Block:** Made from either eco-friendly EVA foam or lightweight, denser foam, Manduka's foam yoga blocks provide a softer, spongier surface compared to cork blocks. Foam blocks are perfect for beginners and those looking for extra cushioning and support during their practice.

### How do I store and care for my Manduka yoga blocks?

To store your Manduka yoga block, keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid storing the block in extreme heat or cold, as this may cause the foam material to degrade over time. When traveling, use a yoga towel or cover to protect the block from dirt and moisture. To clean your yoga block, use a damp cloth and mild soap or yoga mat cleaner, as mentioned earlier in the FAQ.
