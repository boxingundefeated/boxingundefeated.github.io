### How thick are Nike yoga mats?

Nike yoga mats come in various thickness options, usually ranging from 3-6 millimeters, to provide a comfortable, stable, and well-cushioned practice surface. The thickness you choose depends on your personal preference and comfort level during practice.

### Are Nike yoga mats eco-friendly?

While some Nike yoga mats are made of TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer), which is more environmentally friendly than traditional PVC, it is essential to note that not all Nike yoga mats are eco-friendly. ### How do I clean my Nike yoga mat?

Avoid immersing the entire mat in water, as this may damage the material.
