### Important Features of Large Yoga Blocks

Large Yoga Blocks are designed to provide support and stability in various yoga poses. Some of the key features to look for when choosing the right set of blocks include:

- Size: The typical dimensions of large yoga blocks are 9" x 6" x 4", but some may vary slightly. Pick a size that suits your individual needs and comfort level.

- Material: Choose yoga blocks made from high-quality materials like foam, cork, or wood. These materials provide optimal support and durability.

- Grip: Non-slip surfaces are essential for practicing yoga safely and effectively. Look for blocks with textured or non-slip surfaces, especially if you tend to sweat during practice.

### Considerations for Purchasing Large Yoga Blocks

Before investing in a set of large yoga blocks, consider the following:
