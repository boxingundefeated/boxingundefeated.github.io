### Pure Copper vs Copper-Plated Water Bottles

Pure copper water bottles are made entirely from solid copper while copper-plated ones have a thin layer of copper over a stainless steel body. Pure copper water bottles are more expensive but provide better health benefits due to their higher copper content. However, they require more care and maintenance to prevent corrosion and staining. Copper-plated bottles are less expensive and require less care, but their health benefits may not be as potent.

### Size and Capacity

Consider the size and capacity of the water bottle to ensure it meets your needs. Copper water bottles come in various sizes, ranging from 250ml to 1.5L. Smaller bottles are suitable for short trips or workouts, while larger bottles are ideal for long trips or office use.

### Leak-Proof Design

Choose a leak-proof design to prevent water from spilling inside your bag or on your clothes. Copper water bottles with screw-tops or silicone seals are generally more secure than those with flip-top caps.
