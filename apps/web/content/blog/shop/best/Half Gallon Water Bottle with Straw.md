### How can I use a half-gallon water bottle with a straw?

To use a half-gallon water bottle with a straw, follow these steps:

1. Remove the cap from the water bottle by twisting it counter-clockwise.

2. Slide the straw into the bottle through the open cap, ensuring that it is placed firmly into the straw slot.

3. Close the cap back onto the bottle, making sure it is securely locked in place.

4. Drink water by placing your lips on the straw and sucking gently, preventing any liquid from splashing out or down your nose.

5. When not in use, keep the cap on the bottle to prevent spills and contamination.

### How do I clean a half-gallon water bottle with a straw?

Cleaning a half-gallon water bottle with a straw regularly is essential to maintain hygiene and ensure the bottle's longevity. To effectively clean your bottle:

- Fill the bottle with equal parts water and vinegar or baking soda solution, ensuring that the straw is fully submerged.

- Shake the bottle well and let it sit for a few hours to loosen any grime or stains.

- After soaking, thoroughly rinse the bottle and straw with water, ensuring that no cleaning solution is left behind.

- To clean the inside of the straw, use a small brush or pipe cleaner to remove any residue or buildup.

- Allow the bottle and straw to air dry completely before reassembling and using it again.
