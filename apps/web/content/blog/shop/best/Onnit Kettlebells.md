### Important Features to Consider

When choosing a kettlebell, there are several features to consider:

- Size and Weight: Make sure the kettlebell size is comfortable to grip and handle. Regarding weight, start with a lighter weight if you're new to kettlebell training or opt for a heavier weight if you have experience.

- Material: Kettlebells come in different materials like cast iron, steel, or rubber-coated. Cast iron provides a solid, robust feel but can be prone to chips over time. Steel kettlebells are a more durable choice. Rubber-coated ones offer better grip and protect your flooring from damage.

- Handle Width: The width of the handle should be suitable for your hand size. Too narrow may lead to discomfort during prolonged use, while a too-wide handle might make some exercises difficult.

### Considerations for Safe and Effective Use

Here are some considerations to keep in mind while using Onnit Kettlebells:

1. Start with lighter weights until you get comfortable with the movements and techniques.

2. Use proper form and technique to avoid injury. Seek guidance from a qualified fitness professional if needed.

3. Allow adequate rest between sets to allow your muscles to recover.

4. Incorporate a balanced workout plan that includes other exercises besides kettlebell workouts.
