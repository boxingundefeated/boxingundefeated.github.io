### How do infuze water bottles work?

Infuse water bottles work by allowing you to add fresh fruits, herbs, and vegetables to the bottle's infuser chamber, which then steep in your water, enhancing its taste and nutritional value. As you sip your water, the flavors from the infused ingredients are continuously released into your drink.

### How do I clean an infuze water bottle?

Cleaning an infuse water bottle is relatively simple. Begin by disassembling the bottle and removing any removable parts, such as the infuser chamber, lid, and straw (if applicable).
