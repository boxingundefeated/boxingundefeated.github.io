### How do I properly pack a small gym duffle bag?

To maximize space and efficiency when packing a small gym duffle bag, follow these tips: \* Start by rolling up smaller items like socks and undergarments, and place them in any available compartments or pockets.

- Place your workout shoes or sneakers in a separate compartment, if available, to keep them away from clean clothes and prevent odor transfer.

- Fill larger compartments with bulkier items such as clothes and towels, ensuring that they are packed snugly to maximize space.

- Place smaller, more valuable items like wallets, keys, and smartphones in a designated, secure pocket to keep them safe and easily accessible.

- Finally, zipped up the bag securely and make sure all straps are adjusted for comfort before heading to the gym.
