### How do I clean my 5L water bottle?

1. Empty the bottle completely and rinse it with warm water and a mild dishwashing soap.

2. Use a bottle brush to clean the inside of the bottle, specifically targeting the bottom and corners where residue can accumulate.

3. Rinse the bottle thoroughly with warm water to remove any soap residue.

4. Allow the bottle to air dry completely before using it again. You can also use a clean, dry cloth or paper towel to dry the bottle if desired.

### How long will water stay fresh in a 5L water bottle?

Water stored in a clean, properly sealed 5L water bottle can stay fresh for several days. However, to ensure the best quality and taste, it is recommended to consume the water within 24 to 48 hours after filling the bottle.
