When shopping for a color changing water bottle, there are several key features to keep in mind:

- Thermal Insulation: Look for water bottles that can maintain the temperature of your beverage. This is especially important if you prefer hot or cold drinks.

- Capacity: Consider how much liquid you'll need throughout the day and select a bottle with an appropriate capacity.

- BPA-Free Materials: Ensure that the bottle is made of safe materials, such as stainless steel or BPA-free plastic, to avoid potential health risks.

- Ease of Cleaning: Opt for bottles with a wide mouth or removable parts for easy cleaning.
