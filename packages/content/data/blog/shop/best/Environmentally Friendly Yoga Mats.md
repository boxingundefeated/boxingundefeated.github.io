### Important Features to Consider

When shopping for an environmentally friendly yoga mat, there are several key features you should look for:

1. Material: Ensure that the yoga mat is made from sustainable materials such as natural rubber, jute, or organic cotton. Avoid mats made from PVC or other harmful plastics.

2. Non-Slip Surface: Choose a mat with a non-slip surface to prevent injuries during practice.

3. Thickness: Opt for a mat between 3mm and 5mm thick for optimal cushioning and comfort during your practice.

4. Size: Select a mat that fits your body size and practice space comfortably. Standard sizes range from 68 inches to 74 inches long and 24 inches to 26 inches wide.

### Considerations for Environmentally Conscious Buyers

There are several factors that environmentally conscious buyers should consider when purchasing a yoga mat:

- Longevity: Choose a high-quality, durable mat that will last for many years, reducing the need to replace it frequently.

- Packaging: Look for mats packaged in minimal or recyclable materials to minimize waste.

- Brand Reputation: Research the brand's sustainability practices and certifications before purchasing to ensure their commitment to environmentally friendly products.

An environmentally friendly yoga mat is typically made from sustainable, biodegradable, or recyclable materials such as natural rubber, cork, or jute. These materials have a lower environmental impact compared to traditional PVC mats.

### Why choose an environmentally friendly yoga mat?

Environmentally friendly yoga mats not only benefit your practice but also help reduce harm to the environment. They are made from renewable resources, and some are even made from recycled materials, thus reducing waste. Moreover, these mats don't off-gas harmful chemicals often found in traditional PVC mats, promoting better air quality while you practice.
