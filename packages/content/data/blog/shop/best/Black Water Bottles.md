### How do black water bottles keep my drinks hot or cold?

Black water bottles are equipped with double-walled insulation or vacuum insulation technology, which creates an airless space between the inner and outer walls of the bottle. This minimizes heat transfer, allowing your beverages to maintain their temperature for longer periods.

### What materials are black water bottles made from?

Black water bottles are commonly made from stainless steel and glass. Stainless steel is known for its durability, resistance to rust, and retention of temperature. Glass, on the other hand, is transparent, easy to clean, and free from any potential harmful chemicals migrating into your beverage. The choice between these two materials depends on your personal preferences and needs.

### How do I clean my black water bottle?

3. Air-dry the bottle completely before storing it

For more difficult stains or buildup, consider using white vinegar or baking soda to help remove deposits and odors. Additionally, some bottles may be dishwasher-safe; consult the product documentation for specific care instructions.
