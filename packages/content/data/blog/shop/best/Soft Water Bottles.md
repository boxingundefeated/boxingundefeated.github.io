## FAQ

### What are soft water bottles?

Soft water bottles are flexible, collapsible containers that hold water or other beverages. They're usually made from materials such as silicone or flexible TPU. These bottles are lightweight, easy to clean, and take up very little space when empty or collapsed.

### What are the advantages of using soft water bottles over hard ones?

- Soft water bottles are lighter and less bulky than hard plastic ones.

- They're more flexible, easier to pack, and take up less storage space.

- These bottles can often be washed in the dishwasher, making maintenance easier.

### Can soft water bottles withstand extreme temperatures?

Always refer to the manufacturer's guidelines on the temperature recommendations for each specific bottle. Generally, silicone and TPU-based bottles can withstand temperature differences but may not be as heat or cold resistant as stainless steel or hard plastic ones.

### How do I clean my soft water bottle?

Most soft water bottles are dishwasher safe, so you can follow the usual dishwasher cleaning procedure. Some bottles may need to be hand-washed; always consult the manufacturer's instructions.

### How do I store a soft water bottle?

Soft water bottles can be stored either full or empty. When empty, they can be collapsed to save space. Store in a cool, dry place to extend their lifespan and maintain freshness.
