## Buyer's Guide

### Important Features of FormFit Water Bottles

When purchasing a FormFit water bottle, consider several essential features:

1. Capacity: Water bottles come in various sizes, typically ranging from 12 to 32 ounces. Choose a bottle that accommodates your daily hydration needs and allows you to stay on track with your water intake goals.

2. Material: FormFit water bottles are available in materials such as stainless steel, plastic (BPA-free), and glass. Each option has its benefits in terms of durability, temperature retention, and environmental impact.

3. Insulation: If you prefer consuming cold or hot beverages throughout the day, look for a water bottle with insulation capabilities to keep your drink at the desired temperature.

4. Lid Style: FormFit offers a variety of lid options, including screw-top caps, flip-top caps, and sports caps. Determine which style best suits your needs and personal preferences.

5. Shape and Handle: Some water bottles have handles or ergonomic designs for easy grip and transportability. Consider your intended use for the bottle (gym, office, travel) when choosing a shape and handle style that complements your routine.

### Considerations Before Purchasing a FormFit Water Bottle

Before choosing a FormFit water bottle, reflect on the following key factors:

- Longevity and Warranty: Invest in a water bottle that offers a long-lasting quality and comes with a warranty, ensuring you get the most out of your purchase.

- Cleaning and Maintenance: Make sure the bottle is easy to clean and maintain, avoiding hard-to-reach crevices and narrow openings that accumulate grime and bacteria over time.

- Compatibility with Accessories: Ensure the bottle is compatible with any accessories you may require, such as water filters, infuser inserts, or bottle protectors.

- Sustainability and Environmental Impact: Opt for a bottle made from sustainable or recyclable materials if you prioritize reducing your ecological footprint.

### General Advice for Using FormFit Water Bottles

To maximize the benefits of your FormFit water bottle, abide by the following recommendations:

- Clean and dry the bottle thoroughly after each use to prevent mold and mildew growth.

- Periodically inspect and replace damaged components, such as gaskets, to maintain leak-free performance.

- Store your bottle in a cool, dry place to prolong its lifespan and minimize odor accumulation.

- Refill your bottle with tap water (if suitable for drinking) to reduce plastic waste and save money on bottled water.
