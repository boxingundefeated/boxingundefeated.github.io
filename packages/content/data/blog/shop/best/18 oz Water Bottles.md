## Buyer's Guide

### Features to Consider

When buying an 18 oz water bottle, there are several key features you should consider:

- Capacity: Ensure the bottle has an 18 oz capacity, which is typical for smaller water bottles.

- BPA-Free Material: Choose a bottle made with BPA-free plastic for your health and safety.

- Leak-Proof Lid: A leak-proof lid is essential to prevent spills in your bag or on your clothing.

- Easy-to-Clean Design: Look for a bottle with a wide mouth for easy cleaning, or consider one with a removable straw or valve system for thorough rinsing.

- Insulation: If you want to keep your water cold or lukewarm, consider a bottle with insulation or vacuum sealing technology.

### Maintenance and Care

Proper maintenance and cleaning can extend the life of your 18 oz water bottle:

- Immediately after use, empty the bottle and rinse thoroughly with warm, soapy water.

- For a deeper clean, use a bottle brush to reach the bottom and interior sides of the bottle, and clean the lid with a wet cloth or sponge.

- Allow your bottle to air dry, or use a clean, dry cloth to wipe away excess moisture before closing the lid and storing the bottle.
