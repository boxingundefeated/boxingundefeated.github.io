1. Opt for a bag with adjustable shoulder straps for comfort and proper weight distribution.

2. Look for a bag with reinforced stitching and high-quality zippers to ensure durability and smooth operation.

3. Choose a bag with reflective details for added visibility and safety during early morning or late-night workouts.

### Taking Care of Your Osprey Gym Bag

To prolong the life of your Osprey gym bag, follow these maintenance tips:

- Clean your bag regularly with mild soap and water, allowing it to air dry before storing.

- Store your gym bag in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and damage.

- Check your bag for loose threads or damage after each use and repair it promptly to prevent further problems.

- Avoid overloading your bag, as excessive weight can cause strain on the materials and hardware, leading to premature wear and tear.
