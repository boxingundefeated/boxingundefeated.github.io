### Important Features of Yoga Blocks

When shopping for yoga blocks, consider the materials, size, and shape of the blocks. Look for yoga blocks made from high-density foam or cork for durability. Blocks come in various sizes and shapes, so choose one that suits your practice and comfort level.

### Considerations when Buying Yoga Blocks

Before purchasing yoga blocks, consider your yoga level and practice style. If you're a beginner or have limited flexibility, thicker blocks may be more suitable.
